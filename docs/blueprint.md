# **App Name**: GENIUS BMW EXPO & STOCK MANAGER

## Core Features:

- Executive KPI Dashboard: Real-time summary of total inventory, showroom occupancy, demo units, reserved vehicles, and weekly delivery targets.
- Visual Showroom Planner: Interactive floorplan mapping positions P1-P15 in a grid with custom rows and premium display zones, featuring status-coded vehicle cards with images and VIN details.
- VN Stock Management: Comprehensive searchable database for vehicles by VIN, registration plate, or model, tracking arrival dates and specific showroom locations.
- Delivery Management Calendar: Advanced scheduling system for vehicle handovers including delivery checklists, assigned sales advisors, and preparation status tracking.
- Vehicle Lifecycle & Status Tracker: Granular state management for vehicles including Stock, Showroom, Demo, Reserved, and Incident reporting.
- Dealership Asset Inventory: Centralized ledger for managing physical inventory such as vehicle keys, Setup cards, dealer tablets, and exhibition accessories.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary palette featuring BMW Blue (#0066b1) and BMW Dark Blue (#1c3f95) for professional branding.
- Background in Light Gray (#f5f7fa) to contrast with clean White (#ffffff) content cards.
- Status Color Coding: Stock (Green), Showroom (Blue), Reserved (Purple), Demo (Orange), Preparation (Red), and Delivered (Grey).
- BMW Red accents (#c62828) utilized for critical incidents and delivery preparation highlights.
- Premium Retail Next-inspired responsive dashboard with a modern card-based architecture and generous white space.
- Technical sans-serif headings for an automotive feel paired with high-legibility body fonts for dense stock lists.
- Thin-stroke, geometric icon sets inspired by modern BMW iDrive and digital instrument clusters.
